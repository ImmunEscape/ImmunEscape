The Concept

ImmunEscape is a scientific escape game, developed in Toulouse, designed to introduce immune system mechanisms in a fun and immersive way. In a room or in virtual reality, experience an adventure inspired by biomedical research.

Who is ImmunEscape for?

Students, teachers, patients, the curious or enthusiasts: the game adapts to all audiences and fits into various contexts such as education, science communication or therapeutic education.

Available Scenarios

  • Immunity against tuberculosis
  • Immune response to pneumococcus
  • Therapeutic strategies in immunodeficient children
